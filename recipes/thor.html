Thor's Mighty Meatballs

How to make:

Channel the strength of the thunder god with these flavorful meatballs. Mix ground lamb or beef with breadcrumbs, chopped onion, garlic, and a pinch of allspice. Form into small balls, pan-fry until golden brown, and simmer in a gravy made with beef broth, brown sugar, and a touch of Worcestershire sauce. Serve with mashed potatoes and roasted vegetables for a satisfying meal fit for a warrior.
