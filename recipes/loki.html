Loki's Trickster Stew

How to make:

Embrace the mischief of the cunning god with this unexpected stew. Combine diced chicken, potatoes, carrots, and peas in a broth flavored with ginger, soy sauce, and a touch of honey. For a surprising twist, add chopped pineapple or mango chunks, representing Loki's shapeshifting abilities and playful chaos.
